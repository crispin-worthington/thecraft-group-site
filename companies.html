Electrical

Craft Electric

Calgary, Alberta

Craft Electric is a full-service electrical contracting company serving residential and commercial clients across Calgary and surrounding areas. From new builds and renovations to service calls and panel upgrades, the team delivers reliable, code-compliant work with transparent pricing.

The company is built on a simple promise: show up on time, do it right, and charge a fair price. Every electrician on the team is licensed, experienced, and takes pride in clean, safe work.

Services Include:

  • Residential wiring and rewiring
  • Commercial electrical installations
  • Panel upgrades and inspections
  • EV charger installation
  • Lighting design and retrofits
  • Emergency electrical service

Landscaping

Brink Landscaping

Vernon, Okanagan, British Columbia

Brink Landscaping serves Vernon and the greater Okanagan region with professional landscape design, installation, and maintenance services. The team combines craftsmanship with practical know-how to create outdoor spaces that look great and hold up to the Okanagan climate.

From retaining walls and irrigation systems to seasonal cleanups and full property transformations, Brink handles projects of every scale with the same attention to detail.

Services Include:

  • Landscape design and installation
  • Hardscaping — patios, walkways, retaining walls
  • Irrigation system installation and repair
  • Seasonal maintenance and cleanup
  • Sod installation and grading
  • Snow removal (winter)
