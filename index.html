What We Do

Real Businesses. Real People. Real Growth.

The Craft Group acquires and operates skilled-trade service companies across Canada. We keep what works, upgrade what doesn't, and give every team member a reason to stay.

🔧

We Buy Good Businesses

We partner with owners of established service companies who want to step back, retire, or grow with support. We keep your team, your name, and your reputation intact.

📈

We Make Them Better

Better pay for your crew. Better systems powered by modern technology. Better marketing to keep the pipeline full. We handle the back office so your team can focus on the work.

🤝

We Take Care of People

Every company in our group offers competitive wages, clear career paths, and a culture where skilled workers are respected — not treated as replaceable parts.
