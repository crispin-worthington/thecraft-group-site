Why Work With Us

What You Get at a Craft Group Company

💰

Competitive Pay — Always

We pay at or above market rates for every position. Your skills are valuable, and your paycheque should prove it. No bait-and-switch, no "we'll revisit it in six months."

🛠️

Modern Tools & Equipment

We invest in quality tools, vehicles, and technology so you can do your best work without fighting your equipment. If something's broken, we replace it — fast.

📈

Career Growth

As we grow, new leadership positions open up. We promote from within first. If you want to lead a crew, manage a branch, or grow into operations — there's a path here.

🏥

Benefits Package

Health and dental coverage for you and your family. We take care of the people who take care of business.

🧠

Training & Development

We support apprenticeship programs, safety certifications, and skills training. Getting better at your craft is part of the job description.

🤙

A Culture That Respects You

No micromanagement. No pointless meetings. Show up, do great work, go home to your family. We trust our people because we hire people worth trusting.
