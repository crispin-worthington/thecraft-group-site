About Us

Built Around Real Craftsmen

We started The Craft Group because the best tradespeople deserve more than a paycheque — they deserve ownership.

Our Story

It Started with a Simple Idea

The best tradespeople are craftsmen — artists who take pride in their work. But most of them are stuck working for someone else, making someone else rich.

What if there was a way to back those people? Give them a business to run, handle the back office headaches, and let them focus on what they do best — then sell them equity in what they build?

That idea became The Craft Group. Founded by Danny Vass, we started with Craft Electric in Calgary and Brink Landscaping in Vernon, BC. Two companies, two trades, one model: find ambitious craftsmen, give them AI-powered infrastructure and support, develop them into operators, and make them owners.

The Vision

"The trades built this country, but the people who do the work rarely own the business. We're changing that. We're building an ecosystem where the best craftsmen become owners — backed by AI, freed from paperwork, and rewarded with real equity. In ten years, every city in Canada will have a Craft Group operator who went from swinging a hammer to running the show."

— Danny Vass, Founder

Our Mission

Turn Craftsmen Into Owners

The trades are full of talented people stuck on someone else's payroll. We're building a network where the best people own what they build — and have the infrastructure to win.

For Craftsmen

A real path from skilled worker to business owner. We recruit, develop, and back ambitious people — then sell them equity in the business they run. Your craft, your company.

For Retiring Owners

A respectful exit that protects your legacy. We buy your business, keep your team, and develop your best people into the next generation of owners.

For Communities

Owner-operators who live in the community and have skin in the game. When the person running the business owns it, the work is better. Period.

Leadership

Who's Behind This

DV

Danny Vass

Founder & CEO

Danny has built and scaled businesses across multiple industries — from a medical clinic startup that sold for $20M to a beverage company that went public at a $70M valuation. He's done manufacturing, construction, cannabis, and tech. But The Craft Group is where it all comes together.

His thesis is simple: the best tradespeople are craftsmen who treat their work like art. They deserve to own what they build, not just collect a wage. Danny founded The Craft Group to create the infrastructure — AI-driven operations, back office support, marketing, recruiting — that lets ambitious people focus on their craft and earn real equity.

He splits his time between Calgary and Vernon, working hands-on with each company's team. His job is to find the right people, acquire the right businesses, and connect the two. Then get out of the way.
